IBM

IBM is a leading cloud platform and cognitive solutions company. Restlessly reinventing since 1911, we are the largest technology and consulting employer in the world, with more than 350,000 employees serving clients in 170 countries. With Watson, the AI platform for business, powered by data, we are building industry-based solutions to real-world problems. For more than seven decades, IBM Research has defined the future of information technology with more than 3,000 researchers in 12 labs located across six continents.

IBM Cloud

IBM Cloud includes infrastructure as a service (IaaS), software as a service (SaaS) and platform as a service (PaaS) offered through public, private and hybrid cloud delivery models. For IaaS, IBM Cloud includes SoftLayer, which provides public cloud infrastructure with a range of automated services. For SaaS, IBM Cloud includes offerings in areas such as customer engagement and digital marketing. For PaaS, IBM Cloud includes offerings in areas such as data analytics, DevOps and application development, as well as management and security.
